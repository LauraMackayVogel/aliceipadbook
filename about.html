Alice Learns to Believe is an original story written and illustrated by Laura Marie Mackay. The concept and research for Alice Learns to Believe started when Laura studied Visual Communication and Child Psychology at the University of Michigan. Laura dedicates this book to her three nieces and nephew: Josie 4, Isabel 7, Sophia 9 and Nicholas 10. She currently resides in San Francisco, California.
